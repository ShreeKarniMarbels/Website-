Stories from Rajsamand & Kishangarh

Rajasthan's marble legacy goes back generations. Rajsamand — often called 'The Marble City' — is famed for its white marble with subtle veins, prized by stonemasons and architects. Kishangarh, a major marble market, is renowned for both indigenous and imported Italian-style marbles traded and finished at scale. Together they supply everything from classical temple work to modern luxury interiors.

How our stone is sourced

We source directly from quarries around Rajsamand and the marketplaces of Kishangarh, hand-inspecting each block and slab. Sustainable cutting and quality-controlled polishing ensure every piece meets premium standards.

Why choose marble & granite?

Marble adds luminosity and elegance; granite delivers strength and longevity. Our range includes polished, honed, and textured finishes, suitable for floors, kitchens, façades and bespoke installations.
